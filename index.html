🌍 WORLD ERAS (FULL EXPANDED TIMELINE)

Mythic Age

Time: Before time itself

Events: Birth of universes, formation of cosmic energy, creation of primordial beings and first gods.

Impact: Foundation of all reality layers and myth systems.

Age of Gods

Time: 2000–400 BC

Events: Olympus rule, Titan war, divine punishment cycles, human creation myths.

Impact: Gods directly influenced Earth civilizations and laws of nature.

Ancient Civilizations

Time: 3000 BC – 500 AD

Events: Egyptian magic systems, Babylon prophecy, Greek mythology expansion.

Impact: First recorded myth systems and temples built for gods.

Medieval Age

Time: 500–1500 AD

Events: Dragon sightings, knight orders, cursed kingdoms, holy wars.

Impact: Myth creatures begin hiding from human kingdoms.

Dark Age

Time: 1400–1700 AD

Events: Vampires emerge, witch hunts, forbidden rituals, shadow societies.

Impact: Separation between human and supernatural worlds.

Victorian Era

Time: 1800–1900 AD

Events: Industrial science vs occult research, hidden monster investigations.

Impact: Scientific attempt to prove supernatural existence.

🧬 MYTHICAL CREATURES (EXPANDED FILES)

Zeus

Type: God

Origin: Olympus Realm

Era: Age of Gods

Abilities: Thunder control, divine judgment, sky manipulation

Story: Supreme ruler of Olympus who defeated Titans and established divine order.

Dragon

Type: Ancient Beast

Origin: Pre-human Earth layers

Era: Mythic Age

Abilities: Fire breath, flight, extreme longevity

Story: Guardians of ancient realms that protected hidden energy cores of Earth.

Vampire

Type: Undead Entity

Origin: Dark European Myth

Era: Dark Age

Abilities: Immortality, regeneration, mind control

Story: Humans cursed by forbidden rituals that became eternal predators of night.

Werewolf

Type: Cursed Human

Origin: Lunar curse lineage

Era: Medieval Age

Abilities: Super strength, transformation, enhanced senses

Story: Humans cursed by moon cycles, becoming beasts under full moon nights.
